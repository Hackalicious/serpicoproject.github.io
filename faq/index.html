              How much does Serpico cost?

              Serpico has always and will always be free. There is no enterprise or limited version.
              To generate revenue for the project we sell a license for individuals, an unlimited license for Enterprise, and support/services.


              How can I contribute?

              There are many ways to contribute:
            • • Spread awareness about the tool (Twitter: @serpicoproject)
            • • Have your company purchase a license (Individual, Enterprise)
            • • E-mail us to say how Serpico helped you (thanks —-at– serpicoproject.com)
            • • Provide translation for our Video Demos
            • • Add to the Wiki
            • • Submit a Pull Request
            • • Submit a Bug


              • What is included with an individual license?

              An individual license is a single user license including e-mail support and Slack Support Channel access. And most of all, our gratitude for your support of the project.

              If you would like a bulk license, consider the Enterprise License


              What is included with an enterprise license?

              An enterprise license includes:
            • • Unlimited activations for the organization
            • • Custom installer for the organization
            • • E-mail support for registered users
            • • Slack Support Channel accesss
            • • Stickers if you would like them mailed to you.
            • • And most of all, our gratitude for your support of the project.


              • Where can I get find answers to questions or support?

            • • Wiki: We try to add the most common questions to the Wiki
            • • Slack Support Channel
            • • IRC Serpico Channel
            • • Video Demos


              • What is the difference between basic e-mail support provided through licensing and enterprise e-mail support?

              Enterprise e-mail support includes an Service Level Agreement on responses and escalations. Basic e-mail support does not include a Service Level Agreement.
