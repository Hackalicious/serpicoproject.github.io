Booting Up the Blog and Support

The first commit for Serpico was February 4th, 2013. Since then we’ve had dozens of contributors, thousands of installs, three appearances at BlackHat Arsenal and a little over 50,000 LOCs written. And that just skims the surface. I am so proud to be a part of something like this.

Up to this point each of us has been contributing in our spare time. Internally we were paying it forward to so many people that helped us. However, recently we’ve hit a tough point. To be able to support the community with something worth using, we need to take a chance in offering a paid option for enterprise customers.

All of the information is on the Services and Support page (https://www.serpicoproject.com/purchase) or send an e-mail to enterprise –at— serpicoproject.com.
